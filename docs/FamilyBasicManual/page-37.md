# BASIC

## Computer music and game sound effects

**Family Basic includes great music functions such as automatic playback of 3 simultaneous sounds, output of sound effects coordinated with the movements of the animated characters, and so on.**

**Please try out and enjoy these functions to their full extent.**

## Let's create a musical performance!

### Let's try to compose "Mary had a little lamb" with the computer

**Instructions:**

Enter `NEW [RETURN]` (After entering the command from above, the previous program will be erased from the memory. Please enter the following program.)

**Program Listing:**

```basic
5 CLS
6 LOCATE 8,12:PRINT "メリーサン ノ ヒツジ"
10 PLAY "M1Y2V7T3:M1Y1V5T3:M1T3"
20 PLAY "O2A6G3F5G:O2R3FCEDCEC:O1F7C"
30 PLAY "A5AA7:RFCFRCO1A02C:FC"
40 PLAY "G5GG7:RECERCO1GO2C:O2CO1G"
50 PLAY "A5O3CC7:RF5AO3C3AG:FC"
60 PLAY "O2A6G3F5G:O2RFRERDRC:FC"
70 PLAY "AAAZ:RFFFRCCC:FC"
80 PLAY "G5#AA6G3:RERGRERC:O2CO1G"
90 PLAY "F9:FCFAO3F7:F5CF7"
```

**Translation Note:**

The katakana symbol between the quotes in line 6 reads as "Meri-san no hitsuji" which is the Japanese title for "Mary had a little lamb." Feel free to modify the text between those quotes.

**Execution:**

Upon entering `RUN [RETURN]` the computer plays automatically the "Mary had a little lamb" song.

**Musical Notation:**

The program plays the melody of "Mary had a little lamb" with the following notes:
- Treble Clef: F, E, D, C, D, E, F, F, F, E, E, E, F, G, G
- Bass Clef: Repeating C notes
- Chord symbols: F, C7, F
